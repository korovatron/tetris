Tetris - Classic Puzzle Game

Experience the legendary Tetris puzzle game right in your browser! This faithful JavaScript recreation of the classic block-stacking game challenges you to arrange falling tetromino pieces to clear horizontal lines. Rotate and position the seven different tetromino shapes (I, O, T, S, Z, J, L) as they fall from the top of the playing field. Clear lines by filling them completely with blocks, and watch them disappear to make room for more pieces. The game gets faster as you progress, testing your spatial reasoning and quick thinking. Perfect for puzzle game enthusiasts and anyone who wants to experience this timeless classic that has captivated players for decades.

This Tetris game requires HTML5 canvas support. Please upgrade to a modern browser to play this classic puzzle game.
